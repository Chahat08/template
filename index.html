Product

Nah-Sanchar IP-PBX Based on Open Source Software

Project

The project proposes a single system that is capable of performing various tasks involved in detection, surveillance and monitoring of Active Fire Locations.

Multi-Modal Framework for Monitoring Active Fire Locations (AFL) and Precision in Allied Agricultural Activities using Communication Technologies

Project

The developed system has helped to integrate various activities of Department of Agriculture like highlighting locations of Active fire Locations on Maps, Providing Navigation to reach out to those locations, providing support for realtime reporting system for field officers and district level officers, use this data to trace defaulters and initiating actions in form of stopping various Government benefits.

Design of Monitoring & Surveillance System for Active Fire Locations in Haryana

Project

A Deep Learning based model works for the identification and alleviation of DDoS attacks in SIP based networks. The system developed here is scalable and highly available with load balancing and failover addressing capabilities. The proposed model obtains a significant improvement in the performance than the previously existing state-of-the-art techniques in terms of accuracy and detection rate.
